The Discipline

Why the risk sits low.

Downside protection is not a feature we advertise — it is the architecture of the entire strategy. Four safeguards stand between our investors and loss.

I

Funds never leave escrow

Every dollar is deployed inside a licensed escrow account tied to a specific closing. The capital funds the transaction and returns — it is never handed to a borrower.

II

Title-verified closings

Every transaction runs through licensed escrow and title. Clear title and controlled disbursement are conditions of every closing.

III

Short duration

Transactions clear in days, not years. Capital returns almost as fast as it deploys, limiting exposure to any single deal, market, or cycle.

IV

AI-assisted underwriting

Machine-assisted analysis screens every file, overseen by a dedicated Chief Underwriting Officer. Judgment and technology, working together.

The Capital Flow

How it works.

A single, disciplined loop — from committed capital to secured loan to quarterly distribution, and back again.

I
Investors · LPs

Capital is committed

Qualified investors commit capital to Rez Gap Plus, LP, becoming limited partners in a fund built for escrow-protected, income-producing private credit.

II
Pinecone Capital · GP

The fund allocates

As general partner and manager, Pinecone Capital directs committed capital toward transactions that meet our short-duration, escrow-protected mandate.

III
Real Quick Funds · Funding Engine

Transactions are funded

Real Quick Funds underwrites and funds each deal — EMD, double closes, Morby / Stack — with the capital held in escrow the entire time. The engine that puts capital to work.

IV
Repayment · Distributions

Funding clears; investors are paid

As each transaction closes, the funding clears escrow and returns to the fund; income is distributed to investors every quarter. The community continually feeds new, qualified deal flow into the pipeline.
