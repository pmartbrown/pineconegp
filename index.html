The Strategy

Capital preserved. Income delivered.

01

Preservation First

Every dollar is deployed as escrow-protected funding that never leaves escrow. We underwrite to protect principal before we chase yield — return of capital before return on capital.

02

Quarterly Income

Short-duration transactions clear quickly and predictably, funding steady quarterly distributions to our investors rather than illiquid, multi-year lockups.

03

Escrow-Protected

No unsecured paper, no speculation. Every dollar sits in licensed escrow tied to a real, title-verified closing — protected from start to finish, never handed to a borrower.

Why the risk sits low

Four layers of protection under every dollar.

Funds never leave escrow

Every dollar is deployed inside a licensed escrow account tied to a specific closing. The capital funds the transaction and returns — it is never handed to a borrower.

Escrow & title protection

Funds move through licensed escrow and every closing runs with title verification. Ownership and clean title are confirmed before a single dollar is released.

Short duration

Transactions clear in days, not years. Capital turns over almost as fast as it deploys, exposure to any single deal is brief, and the book stays liquid and responsive.

AI-assisted underwriting

Every deal is screened by AI-assisted models and signed off by a Chief Underwriting Officer — a 30-year insurance discipline applied to private credit.

How it works · The capital flow

From commitment to distribution.

1

Investors commit

Qualified LPs subscribe to Rez Gap Plus through official 506(b) offering documents, committing capital to the fund.

2

Pinecone allocates

Pinecone Capital, as GP and manager, allocates that capital across a diversified book of vetted, escrow-protected funding deals.

3

Real Quick Funds originates

Real Quick Funds underwrites and funds each short-duration transaction — EMD, double closes, Morby / Stack — with the capital held in escrow the entire time.

4

Funding clears & distributes

Each transaction closes, the funding clears escrow and returns to the fund; proceeds flow to investors as quarterly distributions.
